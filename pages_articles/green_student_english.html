Website mock-up for Green Student

Green Student is a student project that addresses the second sustainable development goal:
Zero Hunger. It involves distributing unsold food from supermarkets in Lannion on the IUT campus
to students in need, with the help of Inter Asso. The Green Student app or website would allow
interested students to view the items available in the reserve and order the ones they want at a
low price, to be picked up on Thursday afternoons
(there are no classes on Thursday afternoons at the IUT).

This is an unfinished project carried out in the first year of BUT (2023-2024), carried out by
Kelline Becquet, Cyléa Campan, Emma Jubin, and myself. We presented this project to LTC, which
offered to support us in carrying it out. Unfortunately, no member was confident or committed
enough to pursue this adventure.

Details of my tasks

- Create specifications for a food project,
- Design a mock-up of the website,
- Create a presentation using Green Student's graphic charter,
- Define the various means of promoting the website.

Skills acquired:

Website mock-ups, project management, pitching in English
