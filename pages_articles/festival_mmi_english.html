In order to showcase our personal and/or academic projects, the MMI department at IUT de Lannion has decided to organise an annual competition at the end of the year for the best student projects among 1st, 2nd and 3rd year students. The jury is made up of professors, but the special prize is awarded by the students. For the launch of the festival in 2024, first-year students are working in groups to organise and promote the event.

Details of our tasks

- Audit the training and communication of existing MMI festivals
- Define the objectives, positioning and identity of the festival
- Produce an editorial charter for communication media and channels
(writing rules, publication frequency)
- Design a communication plan and press release
- Create story prototypes for festival communication
- Design a poster proposal with festive motifs in the Memphis style.

Acquired : Collaboration, auditing, project management, design

Tools : Adobe Illustrator, Google Docs, Canva
