The fictional web magazine Iutopie

Our class imagined a web magazine describing the daily life of students in 2050 at the IUT with future technological innovations. My partner chose to write an article about what fashion trends would be like. The aim is to develop a narrative in the article, illustrate it with digital media, and ensure the article is well referenced on the web magazine (SEO).

Logo designed by Alan Quéré

Details of our tasks

- Research and writing of the concept and outline of the article
- Writing the article, title, lead and introduction
- Proofreading and spelling correction
- Enrichment with backlinks and generic niche keywords
- Creation of a photomontage

Skills used: NNarration, SEO, photo editing
Tools : Adobe Photoshop, Google Docs
