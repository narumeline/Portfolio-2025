Graphic design

There are several categories of graphic design, including visual identity. To create a brand's visual identity, you need to define a logo, typography, colours, and the types of images/icons used. The graphic designer must be familiar with the brand (its identity, values and target audience) and analyse the visual identity of the brand's competitors in order to create a mood board. Visual identity ensures graphic consistency across all communication media.
